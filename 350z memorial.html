My 350z (Daisy)

A tribute to a car that meant so much to me

This was the first photo I ever took of Daisy. I had a friend drive it to the gas station due to my lack of a license. Also, I remember only spending 40 bucks to fill up—such a memory!

This is when I installed my favorite set of wheels. Daisy was more than just a car to me.

My first real road trip with her—I remember being so happy; I didn't mind filling up twice.

Exhaust, exhaust! I remember wanting a loud car so badly, but I didn’t want it to be annoyingly loud, so I spent the extra coin.

I purchased a fancy camera to get better pics of her, but sadly didn’t get too many before the accident.
